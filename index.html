SCREAM .. the best horror film to exist



a piece by Estephany Barrera



"Do you like scary movies?" The horror, the mystery, the plot twist, what isn't there to love about the cult classic Scream. I never fully watched the original Scream till I went to a drive in movie theater, once I watched it I was hooked. I think that the first kill, Casey (Drew Barrymore), really reels you in. This first kill made it so you did't go through such a slow burn. Not only that, they hired a famous actress reel you in even more only to see her killed off in the first 5 minutes. That's what I like about Scream, they don't drag it on, they get to the point. Till the ending plot twist that you wouldn't have expected. I'll make sure not to spoil.





I've gotten to the point where I've seen it enough times to appreciate Wes Cravens details. I've watched the recent Scream 2022 film that just came out it paid homage to Craven. I enjoyed the film but let's be honest it's just a reboot. Scream (1996) is a film that is great on it's own. The killers weren't one sided like the ones in the following films. I mean come one, if we didn't make it to the 3rd film we wouldn't have had to see Courtney Cox's terrible mini bangs (poor woman). But, we continued to watch because the main protagonist, Sidney (Neve Campbell), always manages to stay the finale girl.

Make sure to read How Scream Saved the Slasher Genre !! It talks about how at the time movies just weren't scary anymore they were all cliches and not taken literally. Scream really paved the way for the next decade.
